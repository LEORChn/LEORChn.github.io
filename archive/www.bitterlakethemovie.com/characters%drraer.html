Colonel Raden Drraer - Portrayed by EZwolf

At first glance, Colonel Raden Drraer is a quiet, calculating and very observing man that knows what goes on around him. Drraer comes from the cold Northern Province of Osgard and is the last surviving member of a proud military family. During the civil war, he fought hard and rose in rank faster than any man in history, obtaining the rank of Colonel within six years. Because he is a keen tactician and a master swordsman, his superiors order him to travel to Bitter Lake. There he has to make sure that when military matters are discussed, he will do whatever he can to claim the position of High-Commander for Osgard.
