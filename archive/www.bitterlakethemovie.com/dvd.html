After its successful premiere last August at Eurofurence 17, Bitter Lake has now made its way to DVD!
We are proud to announce that everyone in the world can now watch the movie from the comfort of their

so everyone can experience first hand how the movie was made.
Don't miss out on this first time ever furry motion picture and order your copy today!

For more information on the PAL (Europe) version or to order your copy directly, visit Blackpaw.de.
